Symptoms of COVID-19

Symptoms of COVID-19 develop between 2-14 days and can vary depending on the individual. Symptoms include:

  • Respiratory infection
  • Trouble breathing
  • Fever - e.g. headache, increase in body-temperature, sweating, shivering
  • Flu - e.g. sore throat, fatigue, runny or stuffy nose

What should you do?

Danish Health And Medicines Authority recommends:

  • Stay at home
  • Wash your hands regurly or use hand-sanitizer
  • Avoid other people as much as possible
  • Cough or blow your nose in your sleeve in stead of your hand
  • Avoid physical contact with others espescially; kisses, handshakes and hugs
  • Be vary of your work-enviroment as well as your home-envirment in terms of how clean it is
  • If you are an elder or afflicted with a disaese be extremely cautious and keep your distance from others

What should you do if you think you are infected with COVID-19?

  1. Does your symptoms indicate that you are afflicted by COVID-19?
    • If they do, call your personal doctor.
  2. If you have severe symptoms
    • Use the 112 help web-application and follow the given instructions.
